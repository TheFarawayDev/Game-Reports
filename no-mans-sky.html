Pros

  • Enormous, procedurally generated galaxy with near-endless exploration
  • Years of substantial free updates (new biomes, ships, expeditions, features)
  • Robust base-building, freighters, fleets, settlements, and customization
  • Full multiplayer with cross-play; easy drop-in co-op
  • Optional VR support on PC/PS5 adds strong immersion
  • Chill, sandbox loop that rewards curiosity and creativity

Cons

  • Core loop can feel grindy and repetitive over long sessions
  • Onboarding/tutorials still leave some systems opaque
  • Inventory/economy micromanagement can be fiddly
  • Performance and clarity vary in VR and on lower-end hardware
  • Story is thin; goals are largely self-directed
  • Co-op chaos can break pacing/mission flow
